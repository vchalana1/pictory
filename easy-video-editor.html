Pictory is Easy to Use

We make editing videos as easy as editing email.

Step 1: Upload video to edit

As the video is uploaded, Pictory's advanced AI engine transcribes the video and converts it to an accurate text representation where the time stamp for each word is stored internally.

Step 2: Edit the transcription 

Select the key sentences that you wish to include in your final video. Remove filler words like, uhs and ums. Edit the transcriptions that will show up as video captions.  

Our AI can help select a set of summary sentences automatically, if you wish to summarize the video.  

Step 3: Customize Brand Settings

We provide a variety of pre-created animations, fonts, and color settings in the form of themes. Choose one of these themes.  Or, create your own custom brand settings. Add your logo. Add your Intro and Outro.   

Your Video is Now Ready to Publish

 Your video is available as an MP4 file, ready to published to your website, social media, or your learning management system
