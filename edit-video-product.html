Easily Edit Videos Using Text 

Pictory leverages the latest in artificial intelligence (AI) to help you easily edit videos using text. We make editing videos as easy as editing an email. 

Upload video to edit

As your video is uploaded, Pictory's advanced AI engine transcribes the video and converts it to an accurate text representation where the time stamp for each word is stored internally.

Edit and summarize

Select the key sentences that you wish to include in your final video. Remove filler words like, uhs and ums. Edit the transcriptions as you wish them to show on video captions.  

Summarize the video to a desired length. Our AI can automatically select a set of summary sentences from the transcription. Easily unselect or select other sentences to include in the summary.  

Customize brand settings

We provide a variety of pre-created animations, fonts, and color settings in the form of themes. Choose one of these themes.  Or, create your own custom brand settings. Add your logo. Add your Intro and Outro.   

Preview, generate, and share video

Do a quick preview of the video to see how it looks. Generate the video and download it as an MP4 file or share it on Vimeo.
