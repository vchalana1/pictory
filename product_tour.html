How Pictory Works?

Pictory leverages the latest in natural language processing (NLP) and artificial intelligence (AI) to automatically create videos from text content

Select input source

Start with the article URL or just input the video script.

View and edit text summary

Pictory's AI engine automatically selects the best summary sentences for your final video.

In this step, you can add or remove sentences that you wish to include in your final video.

View and edit video story board 

Pictory's AI engine automatically selects the best images and video footages representing the summary sentences. These visuals are royalty-free and licensed from the world's best stock library - ShutterStock.

It also auto-picks the images used inside the article itself.

You can easily change these visuals with a single click and even use your own assets.

Edit audio

Choose from a variety of musical soundtracks to accompany your video or choose our AI-enabled voice-over.

Our voice-over list incudes a variety of natually sounding voices that narrate the text while the video is playing back.    

Choose video theme and customize brand settings

We provide a variety of pre-created animations, fonts, and color settings in the form of themes. Choose one of these themes. 

Or, create your own custom brand settings.  

Preview, generate, save, and share video

Do a quick preview of the video to see how it looks.

Generate the video, and download it as an MP4 file.

Share it on your YouTube channel directly from Pictory.
