How Pictory Works?

Pictory automatically extract bite-sized key messages for social from your blogs and webinar transcripts.

For each message, Pictory creates a brand-compliant short video using the most relevant visuals from a licensed library of millions of media files.

Pictory automatically extracts many key sections from your recorded webinars. 

Bulk export the social posts in a spreadsheet format. Schedule them to your various social media accounts using Hootsuite.

Where to use Pictory?

  1. Repurpose your blog to create dozens of social media posts paired with videos
  2. Create many short video highlights from a recorded webinar
  3. Summarize your recorded webinars
  4. Use our HootSuite integration to easily plan and schedule the posts on social channels
