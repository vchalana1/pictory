Edit video using text in minutes

Video creation has become very easy these days. With omnipresent webcams and smartphone cameras and apps like Zoom and Camtasia, it is really easy to record your talking head or even create a screen recording or a combination of the two. It is easy to record demo sessions. It is easy to record meetings. As a result, I am sure many of us are sitting on piles and piles of video recordings waiting to be shared with world.

Sharing or publishing these "raw" recordings, however, is not ideal. There may be parts of the recording that you wish to cut out because it may not be useful or interesting for the viewer. There may be confidential or sensitive information that you wish to remove. There may also be many fillers like ahs and ums that do not sound professional and that you wish to remove. The recorded demo may not have worked perfectly the first time and you wish the edit out the first take.

You also suspect that most people watching your video on LinkedIn or other social media platforms might be watching it on mute, just like you do. In fact, according to recent research, 85% of the videos on social media are watched on mute! So you'd like to add closed captions or subtitles to your video.

Additionally, according to your company's brand guidelines, a published video must include the company's logo and a standard intro and outro sequence. Plus the closed caption fonts and colors much match your company's brand fonts and colors.

All of this is a lot of work and you may choose to delegate it. You may have an in-house video team who could take on the work of editing some of your important video recordings. Or you may have access to an external video editor that you found on Fiverr or Upwork. According to a survey that we recently conducted, the turnaround time to make your video production-ready is least 7 days in which the video goes through at least 2 or more iterations.

Or, you may decide to roll up your sleeves and do-it-yourself. After all, how hard can video editing be! There are so many free software and services available for video editing. So you start editing your video yourself only to realize that even simple things like removing sections where you were saying something that did not make sense or where you said the first um is painstaking. You have identify the precise start and end timing of each of those utterances and then select that clip and delete it. To get the captions, you may first have to send the video to some transcription service first. Then you have to edit the transcript and align it with the video painstakingly.

Either of these options do not seem very appealing after your first two or three videos editing projects. You decide to publish only the most important videos and leave the others to gather dust.

Well, until now! Now you have a new option to process all your videos.

Pictory is a recently released online software service that leverages AI to help make video editing fast and easy. Pictory is trying to make editing videos as easy as editing email in 3 easy steps.

Step 1: Upload the video edit. As the video is uploaded, Pictory's advanced AI engine transcribes the video and converts it to an accurate text representation where the time stamp for each word is stored internally.

Step 2: Edit the transcription. Select the key sentences that you wish to include in your final video and remove the un-needed sections. Remove filler words like, uhs and ums. Edit the transcriptions that will show up as video captions. Our AI can also help select a set of summary sentences automatically, if you wish to summarize the video.

Step 3: Customize Brand Settings. We provide a variety of pre-created animations, fonts, and color settings in the form of themes. Choose one of these themes. Or, create your own custom brand settings. Add your logo. Add your Intro and Outro.
